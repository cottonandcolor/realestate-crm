# Real Estate CRM — How It Works

A practical guide for daily use.  
**Live app:** https://realestatecrm-ochre.vercel.app

---

## What this CRM is for

This CRM helps you manage:

- **Leads** — people you’re working toward a deal (daily calls, open houses, referrals)
- **Contacts** — your long-term network (birthdays, holidays, vendors, past clients)
- **Listings** — properties for sale or rent
- **Lease Listings** — active leases you’re tracking
- **Tasks** — to-dos organized by project
- **Activity** — a log of calls, notes, emails, and showings across the CRM

---

## Your daily workflow (recommended)

This is the approach that fits how you’re using the CRM:

### 1. When you get calls → add them to **Leads**

Each day, as calls come in:

1. Open the **Leads** tab
2. Click **+ Add lead**
3. Enter at least a **Name** (email and phone are optional)
4. Set **Contact by** if you know when to follow up (e.g. next week)
5. Pick a **Stage** (usually **New** for fresh calls)

You do **not** need the person in Contacts first. Add them directly as a lead.

### 2. Follow up when **Contact by** arrives

At the top of the **Leads** tab, a yellow banner shows **leads to contact today** (including overdue).

- Click a **name** in the banner → phone/email expand inline; the table row highlights
- Click **Notes & activity** → open the side panel to log a call or note
- Update **Stage** (e.g. New → Contacted → Qualified) as you progress
- Set a new **Contact by** date for the next follow-up

### 3. Keep **Contacts** for relationship reminders

Use the **Contacts** tab for people who are **not** active sales leads, or for personal touchpoints:

- Birthday wishes
- Holidays and thank-you notes
- Annual check-ins with sphere of influence

On each contact, use the **🔔 reminder** button to set a date, time, and note.

**Rule of thumb:**

| Situation | Where to put it |
|-----------|-----------------|
| “I need to follow up on a possible deal” | **Leads** → Contact by |
| “Wish them happy birthday / holiday” | **Contacts** → Reminder |
| “They’re a real client now, not a prospect” | Mark lead **Closed**; keep them in **Contacts** |

You can **link** a lead to a contact later (side panel or **Link contact** on the lead row) when it’s the same person.

## Tab-by-tab guide

### Overview

Dashboard home. Shows counts (leads, contacts, listings, open tasks) and shortcuts to each tab.

Also includes:

- **Google Calendar** connect (optional)
- **Import listings** (CSV)
- **Export data** (CSV downloads)

If any **contact reminders** are due today, a red/purple banner appears at the top of the dashboard with acknowledge/dismiss options.

---

### Leads

Your **sales pipeline** — especially for daily inbound calls.

| Column / feature | What it does |
|------------------|--------------|
| **Name** | Click to open **Notes & activity** side panel |
| **Date added** | When the lead was created |
| **Contact by** | Date you plan to follow up (dropdown — saves automatically) |
| **Email / Phone** | Optional contact info |
| **Stage** | New → Contacted → Qualified → Closed → Lost |
| **Tags** | Labels (e.g. Buyer, Open House) — edit via **Edit** |
| **Contact** | Link to someone in your Contacts list |
| **Edit** | Change name, email, phone, tags, stage |
| **Notes** | Open side panel for activity log |
| **Delete** | Remove the lead (permanent) |

**+ Add lead** — only **Name** is required.

**Contact today banner** — lists leads whose **Contact by** date is today or earlier.

**Export CSV** — download all leads.

#### Side panel (Notes & activity)

Opens from a lead **name**, **Notes** button, or **Notes & activity** in the banner.

Use it to:

- Log calls, emails, showings, and notes
- Link or unlink a **Contact**
- See full lead details (email, phone, tags, source)

---

### Contacts

Your **address book** and **relationship** list.

- **+ Add Contact** — first name required; optional email, phone, company, tags, birthday, address, reminder
- **Search** — find by name, email, phone, company, tags
- **Groups** — filter by tag labels
- **🔔 Reminder** — set a future date/time and note (birthdays, holidays, check-ins)
- **Edit / Delete** — update or remove a contact

Reminders set here appear in:

- **Tasks** tab → **Upcoming Reminders** banner
- **Dashboard** top banner when due (with acknowledge options)

Leads linked to a contact show under that contact’s detail.

---

### Listings

Property listings (sale/rent). Import via **Overview → Import Listings** or view the grid here. Export available from Overview.

---

### Lease Listings

Track leases (property, start/end dates, contacts, type: Landlord / Tenant Rep / Tenant).

- **+ Add lease** — create entries manually
- **Edit** — update any row
- **75-day alert** — banner at top when a lease ends within 75 days
- Data is stored in your browser (local) for this tab unless synced elsewhere

---

### Tasks

Your **to-do list**, organized by **project**.

- **By project** — tasks grouped under project headings; drag to reorder
- **Board** — Kanban view (To Do / In Progress / Done)
- **+ Add project** / **+ Add task** — create new items
- **Upcoming Reminders** — contact reminders from the Contacts tab (next 5, overdue highlighted)

Set contact reminders on the **Contacts** tab; view what’s coming up here alongside your tasks.

---

### Activity

A combined feed of all logged activity (calls, emails, showings, notes) across leads and contacts.

## Lead stages explained

| Stage | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **New** | Just added; not yet reached |
| **Contacted** | You’ve been in touch |
| **Qualified** | Real interest; worth pursuing |
| **Closed** | Won the deal (or converted) |
| **Lost** | Not moving forward |

Change stage anytime from the **Stage** dropdown on the lead row.

---

## Leads vs Contacts — quick reference

```
Daily calls & follow-ups     →  LEADS (Contact by + Stage)
Birthdays & holidays         →  CONTACTS (Reminder)
Same person, both hats       →  Lead + Link contact
Deal done                    →  Stage = Closed, keep in Contacts
```

## Tips

1. **Start simple** — name + Contact by on a lead is enough for most follow-ups.
2. **Log calls in Notes** — open the side panel after a conversation so you remember what was said.
3. **Don’t duplicate** — prospects go in Leads; use Contacts when it’s about the relationship, not the deal.
4. **Check Leads first** each morning — the **contact today** banner tells you who to call.
5. **Check Tasks** for contact reminders — birthdays and holidays you set on Contacts show there.
